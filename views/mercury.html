Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and orbits around 36 million miles away from the Sun. A year on mercury, the time to orbit the Sun, is only around 88 days. It is also the smallest planet in the solar system, with a diameter of only around 1515 miles and the mass of only 0.06 Earths.
